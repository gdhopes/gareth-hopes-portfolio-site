The Problem

Running can be difficult... There are a lot of things to consider, from how to perfect your foot strike, right down to how to breathe properly. For your average runner it can often feel like... "am I doing this right...am I doing more harm than good?" During 2020 with the pandemic affecting exercise habits across the globe, running became the go-to exercise, as all you need is yourself and a pair of trainers. Fitness was a hot topic. The problem is that the online world is saturated with generic advice and if the content doesnâ€™t speak to you directly it can be easy to feel disheartened or even worse, cause an injury.

'fitness apps' google trend search 2020

During March and April during 2020 especially, the search term was at it's strongest point.

The Solution

To provide a platform where running progress is a personalised experience, tailored specifically to the user, offering them a personal coach along with a motivational community experience. We will know this to be true when our users subscribe to our coaches, or create their own communities and they achieve their running goals in a more efficient manner by doing so.

The Competition

My first step was to take a look at the competition, to see what services other running apps provided and to gauge what opportunities were available. Through this process, it became clear that although there is a lot of professional advice available, none of it is personal to the user. The advice offered is generic, leaving a gap in the market for my product.
