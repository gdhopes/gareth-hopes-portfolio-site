About Me

My Story

I am a curious and creative UX Designer with a background steeped in Customer Service. I’ve recently made the transition from solving customers problems to… well, helping to solve customer's problems… Having worked in Customer Service for several years I’d encountered a lot of frustration along with an understanding of the place where this frustration comes from. It made perfect sense to take what I’d learnt, pack up my skills and journey over from the world of service to the world of design, giving me much more of an opportunity to really help the customer, and their experience.
